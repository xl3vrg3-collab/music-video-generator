## Why these prompts will work better

Against the 10-agent panel's findings:

- **Subject motion** (Reviewer 2): every regen prompt opens with TB's body as the SUBJECT of a verb ("Bear's arms fling", "Bear bursts", "Bear walks"). Kling biases toward whatever is named first.
- **Face shots** (Reviewer 8): 8.2, 9.2, 9.5 explicitly specify "face clearly visible" / "front-facing" to break the 14/17 back-of-head parade.
- **Composition** (Reviewer 4): 7.3 explicitly forbids "centered mandala", 4.1 removes radial symmetry, breaking the bullseye pattern.
- **Character drift** (Reviewer 1): close-ups (4.3) add "one bear only, no duplicates" guard + "chibi" re-anchor. Wide action shots (4.1, 4.2) won't get re-rendered; those were OK on motion.
- **Anime aesthetic** (Reviewer 6): every prompt ends with "cel shading" to fight the 3D-plushie drift seen on close-ups.
- **V2 story break** (Reviewer 9): 5.1/5.2/5.3 explicitly say "NO rooftop, NO railing" to break the V2-on-rooftop geographic glitch.
- **Env consistency** (Reviewer 7): scene 4 regens keep the neon-shard city locked; all clips share "neon shard" or "data wall" language.
